SPARK

Made by Group 5

Space Pioneers and Adventurers for Radiant Knowledge

Welcome to the SPARK website! In this ever-evolving platform, space exploration is not just a dream but an exciting challenge for us to explore together. This web game is designed to provide an interactive and immersive experience of the wonders of the vast universe. During your journey, you will learn a lot about planets, stars, galaxies, and other space phenomena. We hope that through this game, you can expand your knowledge of outer space while enjoying the strategic challenges it offers. Become a space exploration hero, and get ready to face the various obstacles that await out there.

Happy playing and exploring!
